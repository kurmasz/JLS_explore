Signal Generator

This element generates one or more complex signals that are sent to input pins of the circuit during simulation.

Signals are specified using a simple textual form (format).

Enter the signal specification, then click on OK to place the element, or Cancel to cancel.

Only one signal generator can appear in a given circuit.

If the circuit a signal generator appears in is imported, the signal generator is made inoperaable. Hence a signal generator can be put into a subcircuit for testing purposes before it is imported without it affecting the subcircuit when it is eventually imported.
