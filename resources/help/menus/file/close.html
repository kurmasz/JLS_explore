Close Circuit

The circuit currently being edited will no longer appear in the editor window and its tab will be removed.

Any open subcircuits of the circuit being closed will also be removed from the editor window.

If the circuit is not a subcircuit, and if any changes were made to the circuit (or any of its subcircuits) since it was created, opened or saved, you will be prompted to find out of you want to save the circuit before closing it.
